About

Stanford PhD, Artifical Intelligence

I'm an Electrical Engineering PhD student in Sebastian Thrun's Group at Stanford, as part of the Artificial Intelligence Lab of Computer Science. My research focus is in machine learning and deep learning, and my preferred application domains are medical and biological systems. My current focus is in dermatology, where I am developing deep-learning algorithms to automatically track and diagnose skin cancers from images of patients. Previous work spans large-scale drug screening, neuroscience, and psychiatry. I have limited availability for consulting and advising.

UT-Austin, Electrical Engineering and Math

I double majored in Electrical & Computer Engineering, and Pure Mathematics. My research at the time was in invisibility cloaking as part of Andrea Alu's Metamaterials and Plasmonics Research Lab . Hook 'em horns.
